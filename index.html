ABOUT BHARATH MOTOCORP

BHARATH MOTOCORP (HQ: Baraut, Uttar Pradesh) is a reputed manufacturer and supplier of high quality and durable E-Vehicles like E-Rickshaw & E-Loader. Since its inception, we have registered steady growth, both in volume and revenue, under the guidance of our efficient and experienced management team. Today, we are present across All Over India through our vast network of Distributors, Dealers and Technical Partners.

We have established our reputation for quality and efficiency in a very short span of Two years. We are among the leading manufacturers and suppliers of E-Vehicles. Our brand "Bulet EV" is a preferred choice among its customers due to its quality, reliability and ease of operation.

Our products are manufactured in a state-of-art manufacturing unit adhering to international practices of manufacturing, processing, safety and environment. With a production capacity of 2000 vehicles per month, our expert team of engineers and technicians is committed to complete customer satisfaction. All our products are Approved by NATRAX, Indore.
